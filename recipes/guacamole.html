Guacamole

Guac is bomb

Very versatile, can be paired with chips, tacos, bread... etc

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados, pitted
  • 2 limes (juiced)
  • 1/4 cup of chopped/minced cilantro
  • 1/2 white onion (or 1 shallot),finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced (or garlic powder)
  • Optional:
    • 1 small jalepeno (pitt seeds for less heat, or sub with red pepper flakes)
    • A dash of olive oil

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Use a fork to mash the ingredients together, slowly turning the bowl as you go along

Bonus

Serve on a thinly sliced, toasted baguette. Then put a slice of steak on top, and eat
