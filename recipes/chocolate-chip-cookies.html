Chocolate Chip Cookies

Nothing better than homemade chocolate chip cookies!

From what little I know about baking, it's important to carefully follow the directions; one deviation could have a very drastic impact on the results!

I personally don't like my cookies to be super sweet, so I dial back on the amount of sugar added. This way, I don't feel as guilty when devouring an ungodly number of cookies in one sitting

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of salted butter (room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup white (granulated) sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all purpose flour (unbleached)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 (half) tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt (or can use sea salt if you like chunky salt)
  • 2 cups of semi-sweet chocolate morsels (or chopped up chocolate)
