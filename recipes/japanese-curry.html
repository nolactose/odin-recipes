Japanese Curry

Quick and easy dish, most prevalent in Korean and Japanese culture

I mealprep mass quantities of this, because I'm too lazy to make it on a regular basis. I could eat this everyday for weeks

Ingredients

  • Your meat of choice (chicken, beef)
  • Curry roux (Vermont Curry, Golden Curry)
  • Vegetables (chopped into small, bite-sized pieces:
    • Onion
    • Carrot
    • Potato
  • 550ml of water
  • A dash of vegetable oil (or avocado oil)

Steps

  1. Stir-fry meat and veggies in the vegetable (or avocado) oil in a dutch oven (or other large pot) for 5-7 minutes
  2. Add water and bring to a boil. Bring down the heat and let ingredients simmer until they are tender (~15 minutes)
  3. Break up the curry roux into chunks/pieces, and stir in over the simmering heat until completely cooked
  4. Serve over hot rice
